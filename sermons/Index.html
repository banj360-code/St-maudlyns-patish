Parish of St. Maudlyns-le-Wold

The Presidential Address

Date: 2nd February, 2026

Subject: A Call for Structural Integrity (and less beans in the Vestry).

It has come to my attention—largely through the medium of several rather pointed letters from the Altar Guild—that the atmosphere during the Sunday Matins has become... oppressive. While the spirit is willing, the physical manifestation of our collective devotion has taken on a certain "gaseous" quality.

Listen to the recorded evidence:

We must strive for a more dignified silence in the pews. If the organ is not playing, one should ideally not be providing a woodwind accompaniment from the third row.

Dictated but not read.
— Rev. B. Bassette (B.A., failed).
